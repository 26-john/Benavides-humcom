HOW TO COOK CARBONARA

BEFORE TO PROCEED

These are the following tools you need in order to cook Carbonara
  • A Pasta Pot to boil your pasta.
  • A Frying Pan to fry, searing, and browing of the dish.
  • A Wooden Spoonfor sauteing the pasta.
  • A Tong to pluck out the pasta.
  • A Spider, NO! Not the insect, it is a kitchenware wherein you drain your noodles.
    • A Measuring Cup to measure ingredients.
  • A Tablespoon an alternative measurement for 1/16 cup.
  • A Bowl to place the pasta after it drained.
  • A Whisk to blend all the ingredients.


INGREDIENTS

Before you start to cook, you need these following ingredients and measurements.

  • 1/2 lb box Pasta of Spaghetti
  • 3-4 slices of Bacon, minced into 1/2 pieces
  • 2 Beaten Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan Cheese
  • A 1/16 cup or a single tablespoon of Grounded Black Pepper
  • A pinch of Salt


PROCEDURES

After all you settled the tools and ingredients, these are the following procedures to cook a Carbonara.

  • Step 1
    • Start to heat the pot and add a pinch of salt to the pan.
  • Step 2
    • Once the water started to boil add the Pasta to the pot.
  • Step 3
    • Put the cheese and pepper together to the beaten eggs then mix it with the whisk until it is thick.
  • Step 4
    • Cook the bacon into a frying pan and saute it with the wooden spoon. Wait the bacon to appear crispy
  • Step 5
    • Once the noodles is soft turn the heat off and use the tong to pluck off the noodles into the bowl.
    • Another option is to pour the noodles into the spider to extract the water.
  • Step 6
    • Put the noodles and bacon into the bowl wherein you mixed the eggs, cheese, and the pepper, then mix it again the heat from the noodles and bacon will cook the egg to form a Carobonara sauce.
  • Step 7
    • You may place the carbonara into a plate to serve your dish
