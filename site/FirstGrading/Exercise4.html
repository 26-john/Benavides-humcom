Introduction

Jollibee is a major Filipino fast-food restaurant chain operated by Jollibee Foods Group (JFC). During 1975, Tony Tan Caktiong started to open a Magnolia ice cream parlor alongside with his families and the store is located in Cubao, Quezon city. Upon demand from consumers, the store later started to offer hot meals and sandwiches. The family agreed to turn the ice cream parlor into a fast food restaurant, which became the first Jollibee outlet in 1978, when food goods became more popular than ice cream. Upon the Rapid development of Jollibee,the business relies on the unique tastes of the Filipino Fast Food industry, which varied from the American fast food business and able to survive upon the arrival of McDonald's in the Philippines in 1981.




International Branches Timeline

Upon the success of launch of Jollibee, the business started to deploy branches worldwide.

  • In 1978, Jollibee Foods Corporation was formed as the business that would run the biggest fast food chain in the Philippines. There were seven branches in Metro Manila by the end of the year.

  • In 1979, Jollibee's first franchised outlet opened in Santa Cruz, Manila

  • In 1986, Taiwan opened the first Jollibee store overseas, which is now closed and the first international branch of Jollibee.

  • In 1987, the first Jollibee branch in Brunei was opened, marking its entrance into the global market.

  • In 1989, the second Jollibee branch in Brunei was opened.

  • In 1991, the first Jollibee bracnh outside luzon was opened in Cagayan de Oro City

  • In 1995, Jollibee gained a successful opening branches internationally from Guam, Dubai, Kuwait, and Jeddah, the United Arab Emirates, and the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

  • In 1996, the first Jollibee branch in Hong Kong was opened and it was a success lunched.

  • In 1998, the first Jollibee branch in California was opened and it is the first branch in the USA.

  • In 2007, the first Jollibee branch in Las Vegas was opened, which makes it the second branch in the USA.

  • In 2008, Jollibee deploy three branches in the USA, which strengthens the US network.

  • In 2010, the first Jollibee branch in Qatar was opened and it was a success lunched.

  • In 2013, the first Jollibee branch in Singapore was opened and it was a success lunched.

  • In 2013, the 100th international branches of Jollibee was opened in Jubail and Saudi Arabia.

  • In 2015, Jollibee reaches 1000th branches when the first branch of Dubai and UAE was opened.

  • In 2015, the first Jollibee branch in Bahrain was opened and it was a success lunched.
